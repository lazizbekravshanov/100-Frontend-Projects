The valley opens

Foreground grasses rush past while distant ridges linger, the classic trick of depth. Each band of scenery moves as a fraction of your scroll, so the world gains dimension without a single photograph.

Motion with restraint

Every transform runs inside a single requestAnimationFrame loop and is batched to the scroll position, keeping the page smooth even on modest hardware. Nothing animates that the eye cannot follow.

And then, nightfall.
