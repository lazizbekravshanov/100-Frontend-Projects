Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about billing, security, and getting the most out of your workspace. Can’t find an answer? Reach our team any time.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Your trial starts the moment you create an account — no credit card required. You get full access to every feature on the Pro plan for 14 days. When the trial ends, you can pick a paid plan or stay on the free tier; nothing is charged automatically.

Can I change or cancel my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from Settings → Billing. Upgrades take effect immediately and we prorate the difference. If you cancel, your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period — no early-termination fees.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Annual plans on the Business tier can also be paid by invoice and bank transfer — just contact sales to set that up.

How is my data kept secure?

All data is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.3 and at rest with AES-256. We run regular third-party penetration tests, are SOC 2 Type II compliant, and never sell or share your information. You can export or permanently delete your data whenever you like.

Do you offer discounts for teams or nonprofits?

Yes. Teams of 10 or more receive volume pricing that scales with seat count, and we offer a 30% discount for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Send us proof of status and we’ll apply the discount to your account within one business day.

How do I reach customer support?

Every plan includes email support with a response time under 24 hours. Pro and Business customers also get live chat and priority handling during business hours. For urgent issues, Business plans include a dedicated account manager reachable by phone.
