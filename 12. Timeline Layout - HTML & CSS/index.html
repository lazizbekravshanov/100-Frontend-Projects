Our Story

A Studio's Journey

From a two-person desk to a distributed design practice — the milestones that shaped how we build products today.

  1. March 2016

    The First Desk

    Two founders, one rented desk, and a single client. We shipped our first landing page and learned that craft compounds faster than headcount.

  2. September 2017

    Ten Clients In

    A steady book of work let us hire our first engineer and formalize a lightweight design system that still underpins our components today.

  3. November 2018

    First Product Launch

    We stopped only serving clients and shipped our own scheduling tool. It reached 5,000 sign-ups in its first quarter without paid marketing.

  4. April 2020

    Going Fully Remote

    The team went distributed across four time zones. Async rituals and clear writing replaced the whiteboard, and our output stayed steady.

  5. August 2021

    Seed Round

    We raised a modest seed to invest in the product line, growing the team to eighteen people and opening a dedicated research practice.

  6. February 2023

    One Million Users

    Our flagship product crossed a million active users. We rebuilt the core on a faster stack and cut median load time nearly in half.

  7. October 2024

    A Second Product

    We launched a companion analytics app, extending the platform and proving that our design system could scale to a whole product family.

  8. January 2026

    Ten Years, Still Building

    A decade in, forty people strong and profitable. The desk is gone, but the belief that careful work outlasts hype is exactly the same.
